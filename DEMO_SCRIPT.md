## 🎤 Anticipated Questions & Answers

### Q: "How accurate is the Solar API?"
A: "Google uses satellite + LiDAR imagery with millimeter precision. In areas with recent imagery, it's 95%+ accurate. We also show the imagery date so users know if trees/structures may have changed."

### Q: "What if someone enters fake quote data to game the AI?"
A: "Great question. The AI is analyzing against benchmarks ($2.73/watt, 25yr warranties). If someone enters fictional numbers, they'll just get a report showing it's above/below market. No gaming the system helps them."

### Q: "Why should installers support this?"
A: "Honest installers love it. It filters out tire-kickers and only sends them qualified leads who already know solar makes financial sense. Predatory installers won't like us—but that's the point."

### Q: "How do you make money?"
A: "Three revenue streams: 
1. Installer referral fees ($200 per closed deal)
2. Premium subscriptions for unlimited quote analyses
3. B2B licensing for utilities and municipalities"

### Q: "What about areas without Solar API coverage?"
A: "Fair limitation. Current coverage is US, parts of Europe, Australia. For uncovered areas, we show an error and suggest using our chatbot for general solar questions. We're working with Google to expand coverage."

## 💡 Pro Tips for Demo

### Before Demo:
- Pre-load the app (don't start from scratch)
- Test internet connection
- Have backup phone in case of crash
- Pre-type the quote in Notes app for quick paste

### During Demo:
- Speak clearly and slowly
- Don't apologize for loading times (it's real data!)
- Make eye contact with judges, not just screen
- Point physically at screen elements
- If something breaks: "This is live API integration—let me show you the screenshot of when it worked"

### After Demo:
- Offer to let judges try it on their own addresses
- Have QR code ready for GitHub repo
- Mention you're open to feedback
